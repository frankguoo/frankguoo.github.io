About iSG Industrial

Bringing Your Innovation to Life, Your Way. With over 12 years of experience iSG Industrial has fabrication and machine shop facilities with laser cutting machines, water jet cutting machines, CNC machines, and milling and welding capabilities. We are experts in metal fabrication including steel, stainless steel, aluminum, and other materials. Our streamlined operation capacity can produce 50,000 rackmount units on a monthly basis at a quick four-week turnaround time. iSG Industrial places great emphasis on maintaining its role as a leader in innovative technology because it enables our factories to quickly integrate the latest technology into our products.
