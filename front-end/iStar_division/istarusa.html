iStarUSA

iStarUSA is the chassis division of iStarUSA Group. We produce a wide variety of server chassis and embedded chassis, from our mini-ITX tower cases to our 9U 50-Bay Storage Server Rackmount Chassis.
Our chassis have thoughtful features and are designed to be easy to use and dependable. In addition, we have innovative chassis, such as our M-series chassis, the world's first trayless 19" server rackmount chassis, which is available in 1U to 4U configurations. Our AV series chassis are designed for audio-video applications and are available in trayless and hot-swap versions. We have a chassis for every need and can also custom-build a chassis according to your specifications or produce OEM/ODM chassis.
